PROPOSE EVENT CENTER - A DEVELOPMENT FOR A PRIVATE CLIENT AT SAGAMU,OGUN STATE

BRIEF INFORMATION ABOUT THE PROJECT

The propose EVENT CENTER is designed for a capacity of 1200 people it is owned by a Business woman who want to invest at a go. The ground floor has a capacity of 750 people and the first floor a capacity of 450 people. The supporting fascilities like the servery and the rest rooms are positioned outside the building but not far from it as well.The hall is well illuminated with adequate windows for ventilation. The first floor has a (void area) gallery for those upstairs to have a clear view of the stage on the ground floor.
