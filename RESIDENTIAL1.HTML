BRIEF INFORMATION ABOUT THE PROJECT

The client is a reputable business man whose aim and purpose is to have a confortable duplex for himself and the family without having much decking on the first floor.As a result of this,we were given the brief and we came up with sketches and presentation drawings after which this particular design was picked by the client for the best fit.after some corrections on the floor plans we finally agreed on this as our final design.
